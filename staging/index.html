About

Hi, I'm Isabelle.

I'm a senior at UC Davis studying Sustainable Environmental Design. I've always loved plants and color, and arranging flowers is where those two things come together for me.

I make every bouquet myself, usually the morning it goes out. If there's a color or flower you want, let me know on the order form and I'll do my best.

The fine print

Policies

Here's how orders work.

Substitutions

Flowers are seasonal, so what's available shifts day to day. If I can't get a specific flower, I'll substitute something of equal or greater value in a similar color. I honor requests from the order form as best I can.

Freshness & refunds

I arrange bouquets the day they go out. Because flowers are perishable, I can't refund an order once it's arranged. If something's off, message me within 24 hours and I'll make it right.

Your information

I use the info from your order form (name, contact, address) only to fulfill your order. I never share or sell it.
