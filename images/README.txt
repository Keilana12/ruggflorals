To swap a photo: replace the file in this folder with a new image of the same filename.
To add new photos or change what appears where: open ../index.html, find the gallery
or hero block, and edit the background-image paths in the <style> section.

Image tips:
- JPG or PNG, ideally under ~500 KB each (use https://squoosh.app to compress).
- Hero is portrait-oriented (roughly 4:5). Portrait is portrait-oriented (3:4). Gallery is square (1:1).
- Photos with strong text overlays still look good — tiles auto-crop to center.
